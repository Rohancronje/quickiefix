Delete your QuickieFix account

QuickieFix Limited (New Zealand)

In the app (instant)

  1. Open QuickieFix and go to Account (customers) or Profile (tradies).
  2. Tap Delete my account and confirm.

Your profile and sign-in are removed immediately and you are logged out on all devices.

By email

Email privacy@quickiefix.store from the address on your account with the subject "Delete my account". We action requests within 5 working days.

What is deleted, what is kept

  • Deleted: your profile (name, email, phone, addresses, business details, push tokens), saved properties you own, and your sign-in credentials.
  • Kept (de-identified): completed-job billing records that tax law requires us to retain — these no longer link to your profile.
  • Reviews you left for tradies remain, attributed to a first name only.

Deleting your account does not cancel obligations that already exist (e.g. unpaid platform fees for completed jobs, or payment owed to a tradie for completed work).
