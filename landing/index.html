Questions, answered

What does it cost?

Nothing for customers — no booking fee, no service charge. You pay the tradie directly, at the hourly rate and call-out fee shown before you confirm. Tradies pay a flat $15 per completed job (first five free) — no lead fees, no commission.

Where is QuickieFix available?

We're launching on Auckland's North Shore first, then expanding suburb by suburb across New Zealand. Joining the waitlist tells us exactly where to open next.

How are tradies verified?

Every tradie is identity-checked with verified business details (NZBN), makes licensing declarations for regulated work, and carries a public rating from real completed jobs. Poor performers lose access to dispatch.

I manage rental properties — how does this work for me?

Tenants report faults from their phone and only your approved tradies are dispatched, billed to your agency, with an owner-ready report for every job. See the property-manager page — founding agencies get the platform free.

Is it live yet?

Founding tradies and first customers are onboarding now. Join the waitlist and you'll get one email the moment we go live in your area — nothing else.
