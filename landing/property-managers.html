How it works for your agency

Set up once, then maintenance runs itself.

1

Load your portfolio

Bulk-import properties and tenants from a spreadsheet. Tenants get an invite and link to their property automatically.

2

Approve your panel

Invite the tradies and trade companies you already trust. Only they ever receive jobs at your properties — on your commercial terms.

3

Faults dispatch themselves

Tenants report in the app (or call you — dispatch from your desk). The nearest approved tradie accepts and everyone tracks the arrival live.

4

Owners get the story

Every job is logged with times, tradie, and outcome. Export per-property owner reports in one click.

And when the phone still rings…

Some tenants will always call the office. Fine — pick the property, describe the fault, and dispatch your panel from your desk while they're on the line. A nice extra, not the main event: most faults never touch your phones at all.
